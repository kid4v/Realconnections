Support Us

REAL Connections welcomes your support. Help us thrive and grow as an integral part of our community. Your gift, large or small, is appreciated.

What can my donations go toward?

In honor or memory of someone

Donations can be made in the name of a loved one.

For the Supported Membership Fund

The Supported Membership Fund provides financial assistance and scholarships for community members with modest incomes wishing to join the Real Connections village.

Help With General Costs

Real Connections is a non-profit organization, but still needs help financing events, activities, and operating expenses.
