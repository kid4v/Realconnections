Become a Member

Ageless living—at home.  Choice.  Convenience.  Personalized service and assistance. Social events to stay engaged.  Support to optimize wellness and the discovery of new ways to enjoy life.  And,  in times of need, the comfort and security of knowing there is one place to call.

Join the village and experience the benefits of membership yourself.

Apply Now

What are the benefits of village membership?

Single-source for trusted resources

Personalized information, referrals and resources via web or telephone

New Member Consultation / Case Management

To make the most of your membership dollar; hospital to home transition

Social Events and Trips

Take full advantage of programs of interest: travel, theater, outings, discussion and interest groups

General Assistance

Whether it's walking the dog, changing smoke detector batteries or air conditioning filters, to moving furniture and help with gardening. Maybe its waiting for a repairman, dropping a package at the Post Office or other errands

Transportation

Rides to the doctor, the grocery store, or the airport, or whatever errands you need to run

Family Caregiver Support

Caregiver resources, arranging or accompanying your family member to medical appointments, support groups

Screened Home Maintenance and Caregiving Providers

Referrals to a vetted network of service providers will enable members to access with confidence a broad array of high-demand services at pre-negotiated or discounted rates.

A village representative will come to your home to answer any questions at no cost or obligation.

Membership Dues

Members have the option to pay dues either annually or monthly:

Individual Membership

$570/yr or $54.50/month; save $84 by paying the full year membership when you join!

Household Membership

$780/yr or $75/month; save $120 by paying the full year membership when you join!

Additional Member (if more than two people)

$180/yr or $15/month extra

Membership dues may be paid by check or credit card
