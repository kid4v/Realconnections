The Application Process

Complete the following form and a representative from Real Connections will be reaching out to you shortly!

Submit Google Form

Complete Brief Interview

REAL Connections staff members will schedule an interview with all applicants who seem like a good fit

Pass Background Checks

We vet all of our volunteers according to the Department of Justice Background Checks, the National Sex Offender Registry, and personal references

Training

All volunteer applicants who meet our guidelines will undergo a brief program orientation and training
